Real, effective, no-excuse dog training

Tucson K-9 Learning Center is proud to offer Tucson's most comprehensive dog training experience under one roof. We offer obedience training, rattlesnake avoidance training, behavior modification, service dog training, therapy dog training, protection dog training, and even narcotics detection training.

We are recommended by several Tucson area veterinarians, including Adobe Veterinary Hospital, Ajo Veterinary Hospital, and Valley Animal Hospital. We are proud to include the Tucson Police Department as one of our satisfied clients!

We offer 2-week and 30-day board-and-train packages. The 2-week training teaches basic obedience. Need to correct bad behavior? Our 30-day training will stop Fido from destroying shoes, barking at visitors and other bad habits.

Both packages include rattlesnake avoidance training.
